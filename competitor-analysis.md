# Competitor Analysis - SisterSignal

## Existing Solutions
- bSafe
- Life360
- Noonlight

## What They Do
- Emergency alerts
- Location sharing
- Contact-based safety

## Limitations
- No real-time nearby responder system
- Depend on personal contacts
- Limited community-based support

## SisterSignal Advantage
- Real-time mutual aid network
- Nearby verified responders
- Active human support instead of passive tracking
