# SisterSignal

## Problem
Women often feel unsafe when walking alone, especially at night or in unfamiliar environments. Existing solutions like calling friends or sharing location are unreliable, slow, and depend on availability.

## User
Primary users are female university students, especially those on campuses or living in urban areas who may need quick, nearby support.

## AI's Role
AI helps match help requests with nearby available responders, prioritizes urgency, and ensures efficient connection between users.

## Solution
SisterSignal is a women-only mutual aid network where users can request real-time help such as escort, pickup, or check-in. Nearby verified users can accept and respond.

## Competitor Situation
Existing apps (e.g., bSafe, Life360) focus on tracking and emergency alerts. They do not provide real-time, nearby human response systems.

## Success Criteria
A user can request help and receive a response from a nearby verified user within minutes.
